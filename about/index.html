Zee Pauli

About

Design for things people use, share, and remember.

Zee Pauli is a designer working across branding, packaging, and physical product experiences — with a focus on work that feels intentional, tactile, and built to last beyond a single launch moment.

Based in the Pacific Northwest, the practice combines editorial restraint with material craft: visual systems, structural packaging, and branded objects that hold up in the hand and in the world.

For new projects and collaborations, reach out at hello@zee-studio.com.
